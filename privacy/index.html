Privacy Policy

Last updated July 18, 2026

moods.computer is built to work without an account, advertising, or the sale of personal information.

Who we are

This policy applies to the moods.computer website and the Moods desktop app, published by Burning Flowers, LLC. It explains what information is processed when you use either version.

Information we process

  • Weather: If the weather feature is shown, the service at geojs.io derives an approximate area from your IP address, and Open-Meteo supplies local conditions. We do not store your location.
  • Ambient video: Videos play through the YouTube IFrame Player API using YouTube's privacy-enhanced domain. Google may receive technical information such as your IP address, device or browser information, and interactions with the player.
  • Website analytics: The moods.computer website uses Google Analytics to help us understand general traffic and usage. The Mac App Store version of Moods does not include Google Analytics.
  • Local preferences: Your selected visual skin and cached site files may be stored locally in your browser. The desktop app stores only window size and position on your Mac.
  • Fonts: The interface may request a font from Google Fonts, which sends ordinary network information such as your IP address to Google.

How information is used

Information is used only to provide the player, show local weather, remember local preferences, keep the service reliable, and understand broad website usage. We do not sell personal information, build advertising profiles, or use information for targeted advertising.

Service providers

The app and website communicate with Google/YouTube, Google Fonts, Google Analytics on the website, geojs.io, and Open-Meteo for the functions described above. These providers process information under their own policies. See Google's Privacy Policy, GeoJS privacy information, and Open-Meteo's terms and privacy information.

Retention and deletion

We do not maintain Moods user accounts or a database of personal profiles. Local preferences can be removed by clearing the website data in your browser or deleting the app's local data from your Mac. Third-party providers retain information according to their own policies and settings.

Your choices

You can block or clear browser storage, disable cookies, use browser privacy controls, or stop using the weather and video features. You may also use the Google Analytics opt-out tool for the website.

Children

Moods is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as Moods changes. The revised date at the top will show when the latest version took effect.
